Flower Gallery

Hydrangea

Hydrangeas are native to Asia and America. They typically show up in the summer.

Lavender

Lavender is a popular flower typically used in herbs and fragrances.

Peony

Peony come in many colors and bloom in early summer or late spring.

Orchid

Orchids have three parts: petals, sepals, and a labellum.

Daisy

Daisies can be found on every continent in the world (except Antartica).

Tulip

Tulips are usually the symbol of the beginning of spring.

Lily

Lilies are typically found in the Northern Hemisphere and have strong fragrances.

Calla Lily

Calla lilies come in a variety of colors such as white and bright pink.

Hyacinth

Hyacinths are native to the east Mediterreanean and have sweet smells.

Azalea

Azaleas have very bright colors but beware, they are very poisonous.

Violet

You can probably spot violets in your own backyard. There are over 500 species.

Dahlia

Dahlias are flowers that can grow up to four or five feet tall!
